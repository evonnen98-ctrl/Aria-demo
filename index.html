Aria — voice-native onboarding companion

Click the widget in the bottom-right corner to start a real conversation. Aria will ask about your role, team and working style, then build a 30/60/90-day plan and send a written summary — all from one conversation.

Try it: speak naturally, or switch to typing at any point.

Multilingual: reply in German or Japanese and Aria switches automatically.

Voice choice: Aria offers to switch to a warmer, calmer, or more upbeat voice at the start.
